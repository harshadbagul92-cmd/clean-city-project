📚 Awareness & Education

Understanding garbage's impact on health, environment and Nashik's future

⚠️ Harmful Effects of Garbage

🦠

Disease Spread

Uncollected waste breeds mosquitoes and rodents, causing dengue, cholera and typhoid.

💧

Water Pollution

Garbage toxins seep into groundwater and Nashik's rivers, contaminating drinking sources.

🌬️

Air Pollution

Burning waste releases dioxins, CO₂ and carcinogens into the air we breathe daily.

🐾

Animal Harm

Street animals ingest plastic, causing painful injuries and premature death.

🌡️

Climate Change

Decomposing organic waste produces methane — 25× stronger than CO₂ as a greenhouse gas.

😰

Mental Stress

Living in dirty surroundings increases stress, anxiety and reduces quality of life.

🗑️ Types of Waste & Dustbins

🟢

Wet Waste

Green Bin

Organic, biodegradable kitchen and garden waste.

  • Vegetable & fruit peels
  • Cooked food leftovers
  • Tea/coffee grounds
  • Grass & flower clippings
🔵

Dry Waste

Blue Bin

Recyclable materials — clean before disposing.

  • Newspapers & cardboard
  • Plastic bottles
  • Glass jars & bottles
  • Aluminium & tin cans
🔴

Hazardous Waste

Red Bin

Toxic materials — handle with care, never mix.

  • Old batteries
  • Expired medicines
  • Paints & chemicals
  • Insecticides
⚫

E-Waste

E-Waste Centre

Discarded electronics with toxic metals inside.

  • Old phones & laptops
  • Chargers & cables
  • Circuit boards
  • Old TVs & monitors

✅ Do's and Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • Segregate waste at the source
  • Always use proper dustbins
  • Compost wet waste at home
  • Report illegal dumping immediately
  • Use cloth or paper bags
  • Spread awareness to neighbours
  • Participate in NMC clean-up drives

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't litter on streets or roads
  • Don't burn waste openly
  • Don't mix hazardous with organic waste
  • Don't dump near water bodies
  • Don't ignore garbage near you
  • Don't use single-use plastics
  • Don't dispose e-waste carelessly

💡 Daily Eco Tips

🛍️ Carry a reusable bag every time you shop — saves hundreds of plastic bags a year.
🚿 Take shorter showers. Less water used means less energy at treatment plants.
🔋 Unplug chargers when not in use — standby power is invisible but real waste.
🍎 Compost kitchen scraps at home — turn waste into nutrient-rich garden fertilizer.
📦 Choose products with minimal or recyclable packaging whenever you shop.
🚴 Walk or cycle for short distances — reduce carbon footprint and stay healthy!
